# ai-resume-bullet-optimizer
A lightweight AI workflow for optimizing resume bullet points.

## Why I Built This

As AI becomes increasingly integrated into professional workflows, I wanted to explore practical applications of generative AI beyond simple content generation.

This project demonstrates how AI can assist with repetitive knowledge work by improving resume tailoring, identifying relevant keywords, and helping professionals better communicate their experience.

## AI Workflow

The workflow follows a simple prompt-engineering approach:

1. User provides an existing resume bullet.
2. User provides a target job description.
3. AI analyzes the job requirements and identifies relevant themes.
4. AI rewrites the bullet while preserving factual accuracy.
5. AI provides keywords and improvement suggestions.

The goal is not to replace human judgment, but to accelerate the editing and refinement process.

# AI Resume Bullet Optimizer

## Overview

AI Resume Bullet Optimizer is a lightweight AI workflow that helps job seekers tailor resume bullet points to specific job descriptions using large language models.

The goal is to improve resume quality while maintaining truthful and accurate representations of experience.

---

## The Problem

Applicants often spend significant time rewriting resume bullet points for every application.

At the same time, Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) look for keywords and relevant experience that may not be clearly represented in a resume.

---

## The Solution

This workflow uses AI to:

- Rewrite resume bullet points for clarity and impact
- Surface relevant ATS keywords
- Suggest measurable accomplishments to include
- Generate interview talking points
- Explain why the revised bullet is stronger

---

## Example

### Original Bullet

> Managed month-end account reconciliations.

### Job Description

Seeking an accountant with ERP experience and strong process improvement skills.

### AI Optimized Bullet

> Performed month-end account reconciliations within ERP systems while identifying opportunities to improve reconciliation efficiency and strengthen financial accuracy.

### ATS Keywords

- ERP
- Month-End Close
- Reconciliation
- Process Improvement
- Financial Reporting

---

## Sample Prompt

```
Rewrite this resume bullet for the following job description.

Resume Bullet:
[Paste Bullet]

Job Description:
[Paste Job Description]

Requirements:

- Improve clarity
- Keep everything truthful
- Add relevant ATS keywords
- Suggest measurable accomplishments if applicable
- Explain why the revised bullet is stronger
```
